From Our Kitchen, To Yours

We believe great baking should be shared. Try these recipes at home and bring a little Bristo magic into your kitchen.

Pastry
Classic Butter Croissants
Makes 8 3 hrs prep 20 min bake
Ingredients
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 7g instant yeast
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 200ml warm milk
  • 30g softened butter
  • 200g cold unsalted butter (for lamination)
  • 1 egg + 1 tbsp milk (egg wash)
Method
  1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, salt, warm milk and softened butter to form dough. Knead 5 minutes. Refrigerate 1 hour.
  2. Beat cold butter into a flat square between baking paper. Refrigerate until firm but pliable.
  3. Roll dough into a rectangle. Place butter square in centre, fold dough over it like an envelope.
  4. Roll out and fold into thirds (like a letter). Repeat 3 times, refrigerating 30 minutes between each fold.
  5. Roll dough thin, cut into triangles. Roll each triangle from base to tip.
  6. Place on baking paper, brush with egg wash. Prove 1 hour. Bake at 200°C for 18–20 minutes until golden.
Baked Goods
Fudgy Chocolate Brownies
Makes 16 squares 15 min prep 25 min bake
Ingredients
  • 200g dark chocolate (70%)
  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 250g caster sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 100g plain flour
  • 30g cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Flaky sea salt to top
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Line a 20cm square tin with baking paper.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a bowl over simmering water. Cool 5 minutes.
  3. Whisk sugar into chocolate mixture, then beat in eggs one at a time. Add vanilla.
  4. Fold in flour, cocoa and salt until just combined — do not overmix.
  5. Pour into tin, sprinkle with flaky salt. Bake 22–25 minutes. Centre should still have a slight wobble.
  6. Cool completely in tin before cutting. This is key — patience makes them fudgy!
