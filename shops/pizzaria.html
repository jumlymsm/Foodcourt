Classic Pizza Dough
Makes: 2 pizzasPrep: 15 min + 1 hr riseCook: 12 min
Ingredients
  • 3 cups bread flour (00 flour)
  • 7g instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 200ml warm water
  • Extra flour for kneading
Method
  1. Mix yeast, sugar and warm water in a bowl. Rest 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt and olive oil. Mix until a rough dough forms.
  3. Knead on a floured surface for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place in an oiled bowl, cover and rise for 1 hour until doubled.
  5. Divide in two, stretch each into a thin round base on a floured surface.
  6. Top with sauce and toppings. Bake at 240°C for 10–12 minutes until golden.
San Marzano Tomato Sauce
Makes: enough for 4 pizzasPrep: 5 minCook: 20 min
Ingredients
  • 400g San Marzano canned tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • Salt & pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves
Method
  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
  2. Add canned tomatoes, crushing them by hand as you add them.
  3. Add oregano, sugar, salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
  4. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning. Stir through fresh basil at the end.
  6. Cool before spreading on pizza bases. Store refrigerated up to 5 days.
