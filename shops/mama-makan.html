Teh Tarik
Classic Nasi Lemak
Serves: 4Prep: 20 minCook: 40 min
Ingredients
  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 pandan leaves, knotted
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 eggs, hard-boiled
  • 100g dried anchovies (ikan bilis)
  • 100g roasted peanuts
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • Banana leaf to serve
Method
  1. Wash rice until water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. Add rice to pot with coconut milk, pandan leaves, salt and enough water to reach 2cm above rice. Cook until tender.
  3. Fry dried anchovies in oil until golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.
  4. Make sambal: blend dried chillies, shallots, garlic, belacan. Fry paste until fragrant. Season with sugar, salt and tamarind.
  5. Serve rice on banana leaf, topped with sambal, anchovies, peanuts, cucumber slices and halved egg.
Beef Rendang
Serves: 6Prep: 30 minCook: 2.5 hrs
Ingredients
  • 1kg beef chuck, cubed
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves
  • 3 tbsp kerisik (toasted coconut)
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Rendang spice paste (blend)
Method
  1. Blend shallots, garlic, galangal, ginger, dried chillies and lemongrass into a smooth paste.
  2. Fry spice paste in oil over medium heat until fragrant and oil splits, about 15 minutes.
  3. Add beef and coat in the paste. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Add coconut milk, lemongrass stalks, lime leaves and turmeric. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Cook uncovered on low heat for 2 hours, stirring occasionally, until liquid evaporates and beef is very tender.
  6. Add kerisik and palm sugar. Continue cooking until beef is dark and coated. Season with salt.
