Super Crispy Fried Chicken
Serves: 4Prep: 20 min + 4 hr brineCook: 20 min
Ingredients
  • 8 chicken pieces (thighs/drumsticks)
  • 500ml buttermilk
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • ½ cup cornflour
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp cayenne
  • Salt & pepper
  • Oil for deep frying
Method
  1. Soak chicken in buttermilk mixed with hot sauce for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).
  2. Combine flour, cornflour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Remove chicken from buttermilk, letting excess drip. Dredge thoroughly in spiced flour, pressing firmly.
  4. Dip back in buttermilk then dredge in flour again for extra crunch.
  5. Heat oil to 170°C. Fry chicken for 15–18 minutes until golden and cooked through (internal temp 75°C).
  6. Drain on a wire rack. Rest 5 minutes before serving.
Smoky BBQ Burger Sauce
Makes: 1 cupPrep: 5 minCook: 0 min
Ingredients
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp smoky BBQ sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Pinch of cayenne
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Method
  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk until completely smooth and well combined.
  3. Taste and adjust — add more BBQ sauce for smokiness, vinegar for tang, or cayenne for heat.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let flavours develop.
  5. Spread generously on toasted burger buns. Keeps refrigerated for up to 1 week.
