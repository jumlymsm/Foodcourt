No-Churn Hokey Pokey Ice Cream
Serves: 8Prep: 20 min + 6 hr freezeCook: 5 min
Ingredients
  • 600ml heavy cream
  • 395g condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Hokey Pokey:
  • 4 tbsp golden syrup
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
Method
  1. Make hokey pokey: melt golden syrup and sugar together in a pot. Cook until golden (165°C).
  2. Remove from heat, add baking soda and stir quickly — mixture will foam up dramatically!
  3. Pour onto a lined tray. Cool completely, then break into chunks.
  4. Whip cream to stiff peaks. Fold in condensed milk and vanilla.
  5. Fold through most of the hokey pokey chunks, saving some for topping.
  6. Pour into a container, top with remaining hokey pokey. Freeze for 6 hours or overnight.
Fresh Strawberry Milkshake
Serves: 2Prep: 5 minCook: 0 min
Ingredients
  • 250g fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 3 large scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 150ml whole milk
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar (optional)
  • Whipped cream to serve
  • Extra strawberry to garnish
Method
  1. Chill two tall glasses in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  2. Add strawberries, ice cream and milk to a blender.
  3. Add icing sugar if you prefer it sweeter.
  4. Blend until smooth and thick. Don't over-blend — keep it thick!
  5. Pour into chilled glasses. Top with whipped cream and a fresh strawberry.
  6. Serve immediately with a wide straw and enjoy!
