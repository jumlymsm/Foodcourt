Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)
Serves: 4Prep: 20 minCook: 40 min
Ingredients
  • 700g chicken thighs, cubed
  • 200ml thick yoghurt
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
Method
  1. Marinate chicken in yoghurt, half the garam masala, turmeric and salt. Refrigerate 1–4 hours.
  2. Grill or pan-fry chicken until lightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Melt butter in a pot. Add cumin seeds and fry 30 seconds. Add onion, cook until golden.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, cook 2 minutes. Add crushed tomatoes and remaining spices. Simmer 15 minutes.
  5. Blend sauce until smooth. Return to pot.
  6. Add chicken and cream. Simmer 10 minutes. Adjust seasoning and serve with naan.
Classic Samosa
Makes: 12Prep: 30 minCook: 25 min
Ingredients
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 4 tbsp oil + extra for frying
  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled & mashed
  • ½ cup frozen peas, cooked
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • Fresh coriander, chopped
  • Salt to taste
Method
  1. Make dough: mix flour, salt, oil and enough water into a firm dough. Rest 20 minutes.
  2. Fry cumin seeds in a little oil. Add mashed potato, peas and spices. Mix well. Cool completely.
  3. Roll dough into thin ovals. Cut in half. Form each half into a cone.
  4. Fill cone with potato mixture. Seal edges firmly with water.
  5. Heat oil to 170°C. Fry samosas in batches for 5–7 minutes, turning, until golden and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels. Serve with mint chutney and tamarind sauce.
